#4000D8W — White Version

4000DX Hidden Nylon Snap Button — White

Hidden nylon snap button designed for panel connections. The female part (4000D81) mounts on the base panel; the male ball part (4000D82) mounts on the face panel. After installation, the 5MM gap between panels creates a clean, fastener-free surface appearance. Ideal for furniture, wood panels, metal plates, and architectural decoration.
