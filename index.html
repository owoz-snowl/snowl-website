About Us

30+ Years Serving the Maritime Industry

In Greek mythology, the owl was the archetype of the goddess Athena — a symbol of wisdom and navigation. SNOWL Hong Kong Limited (founded in 1995) chose the owl as its trademark, representing the crystallization of human wisdom in every product.

We specialize in OWOZ series marine fasteners, deck hardware, and maritime accessories. Our products are trusted by boat manufacturers, yacht builders, and maritime suppliers worldwide.
